# Social Media Platform Strategy

## Algorithm Signal Weights by Platform

### Instagram (Meta, 2024)
```
Ranking signals (approximate priority):
1. Saves           — highest weight (signals bookmark-worthy value)
2. Shares          — re-shares to Stories or DMs
3. Comments        — especially meaningful/long comments
4. Likes           — lowest weight of engagement types
5. Watch time      — for Reels: completion rate and replays
6. Relationship    — DMs, tags, search for account
```
- Reels reach non-followers; static posts are mostly follower-served
- Engagement rate benchmark: >3% good, >6% excellent

### TikTok
```
Ranking signals:
1. Watch time / Completion rate  — #1 signal by far
2. Shares                        — highest distribution signal
3. Comments                      — especially replies and stitches
4. Likes                         — tertiary signal
5. Profile visits from video     — signals strong interest
```
- First 3 seconds determine if video is served further
- Algorithm tests each video to small batch (200–500 users), then expands if metrics hold
- Completion rate >70% = algorithmic boost

### LinkedIn
```
Ranking signals:
1. Dwell time           — seconds spent reading (unique to LinkedIn)
2. Comments             — especially substantive, multi-sentence
3. Shares               — re-posts without commentary = less weight
4. Reactions            — Insightful/Love > Like
5. Early engagement     — first 1–2 hours are critical for distribution
```
- Posts with links in comments (not post body) get more reach
- Native documents (carousels as PDFs) outperform external links
- Optimal length: 900–1,200 characters for max dwell time

### YouTube
```
Ranking formula:
Distribution = CTR × Watch Time × Session Time
```
- CTR (click-through rate on thumbnail/title): benchmark 4–10%
- Average view duration: aim for >50% of video length
- Session time: videos that keep users on YouTube are boosted
- Suggested feed (70% of views) driven by: watch history match + CTR potential

### Twitter/X
- Engagement within first hour critical for trending
- Replies > retweets > likes for distribution
- Long-form posts (premium) get algorithmic boost over short posts
- Media (images, video) gets ~2× organic reach vs. text-only

## Optimal Posting Frequency
| Platform | Minimum | Optimal | Max before diminishing returns |
|----------|---------|---------|-------------------------------|
| Instagram (Feed) | 3×/week | 5×/week | 1×/day |
| Instagram (Reels) | 3×/week | 5–7×/week | 2×/day |
| TikTok | 1×/day | 1–3×/day | 4×/day |
| LinkedIn | 2×/week | 4×/week | 1×/day |
| YouTube | 1×/week | 2–3×/week | 1×/day |
| Twitter/X | 3×/day | 5–10×/day | No cap |

## Cross-Platform Content Adaptation
- Repurpose: long YouTube → TikTok/Reels clips → LinkedIn carousel → blog post
- Format natively: vertical 9:16 for TikTok/Reels, square 1:1 for feed, 16:9 for YouTube
- Caption length: TikTok (<150 chars), LinkedIn (150–300 chars for hook), YouTube (max 5,000 chars)
