# Social Sciences

This centroid covers the study of human society, behavior, institutions, and
economic systems. It includes business, finance, law, marketing, and psychology.

## Agents
- Economics: micro/macroeconomics, behavioral economics, trade, monetary policy
- Finance: portfolio management, risk management, derivatives, accounting, valuation
  - Portfolio Management: MPT, asset allocation, performance attribution
  - Risk Management: VaR, Expected Shortfall, credit risk, hedging, Basel IV
  - Derivatives: options, futures, forwards, Greeks, Black-Scholes
  - Quantitative Finance: GARCH, factor models, Monte Carlo, time series
  - Accounting: financial statements, ratio analysis, DCF, WACC
- Marketing: SEO, Google Ads, Meta Ads, social media, content strategy, analytics
- Business: business plans, startups, strategy, operations, pricing
- Law: contracts, public procurement, tenders, LCSP, regulatory compliance
- Psychology: behavioral science, cognitive biases, decision making

- User asks about money, investment, portfolio, VaR, stocks, bonds

- User asks about business plans, startups, revenue, pricing
