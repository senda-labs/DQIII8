# Contract Law Fundamentals

## Definition
Contract law governs legally binding agreements between parties. A contract is an agreement that the law will enforce, creating obligations that each party must fulfill. It is the foundation of commercial transactions, employment, real estate, and most business relationships.

## Core Concepts

- **Elements of a Valid Contract:**
  1. Offer: A clear proposal to enter into an agreement on specific terms.
  2. Acceptance: Unequivocal agreement to the offer's terms (mirror image rule).
  3. Consideration: Something of value exchanged by each party (money, services, promise to act or refrain).
  4. Capacity: Parties must be legally competent (not minors, not mentally incapacitated).
  5. Legality: The contract's purpose must be legal.
  6. Intention: Parties must intend to create legal relations (not social promises).
- **Types of Contracts:** Express (explicit terms, written or oral), implied (from conduct or circumstances), bilateral (both parties obligated), unilateral (one party promises in exchange for an act), executory (not yet fully performed), executed (fully performed).
- **Breach of Contract:** Failure to perform a contractual obligation. Material breach: substantial non-performance, gives other party right to terminate and sue for damages. Minor breach: partial non-performance.
- **Remedies:**
  - Compensatory damages: Put the non-breaching party in the position they would have been in.
  - Expectation damages: Benefit of the bargain.
  - Consequential damages: Foreseeable losses from breach (Hadley v. Baxendale rule).
  - Specific performance: Court orders actual performance (used for unique goods/property).
  - Rescission: Contract cancelled, parties restored to pre-contract position.
- **Contract Defenses:** Misrepresentation, fraud, duress, undue influence, mistake (mutual/unilateral), unconscionability, statute of frauds (certain contracts must be written).
- **Key Clauses:** Indemnification, limitation of liability, force majeure, arbitration, choice of law, confidentiality (NDA), IP assignment.

## Common Contract Types
- NDA (Non-Disclosure Agreement), SaaS terms of service, employment agreements, consulting contracts, purchase orders, leases, partnership agreements.

## Practical Applications
- **SaaS businesses:** Standard terms of service, DPA (Data Processing Agreements) for GDPR compliance.
- **Employment:** Offer letters, non-competes (enforceability varies by jurisdiction), IP assignment.
- **Freelance/consulting:** Scope of work, payment terms, IP ownership, kill fees.
- **Fundraising:** Term sheets, subscription agreements, SAFEs, convertible notes.
