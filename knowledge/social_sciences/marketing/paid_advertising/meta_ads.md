# Meta Ads (Facebook & Instagram)

## Bidding and Pricing
```
CPM = (Ad spend / Impressions) × 1000
CPC = Ad spend / Clicks
CPR = Ad spend / Results (cost per result)
```
- Meta uses auction: bid × estimated action rate × ad quality = total value
- Advantage+ (formerly auto bidding): Meta optimizes within budget
- Manual bid cap: set max CPM/CPC, limits delivery at competitive auctions

## Audience Targeting

### Core Audiences
- Demographics: age, gender, location, language, education, job title
- Interests: pages liked, app usage, content interaction
- Behaviors: purchase behavior, device usage, travel

### Custom Audiences
- Customer list (email/phone upload → hashed matching)
- Website visitors (Meta Pixel / CAPI-based)
- App activity
- Video viewers, Instagram engagers, Lead Form openers

### Lookalike Audiences
```
Lookalike = Meta finds users statistically similar to your source audience
Size: 1% (most similar) to 10% (broader reach) of country population
1% lookalike from 1,000+ quality seed = strong signal
```

## Ad Formats
| Format | Placements | Best for |
|--------|-----------|---------|
| Single image | Feed, Stories, Reels | Brand awareness, simple message |
| Single video | Feed, Stories, Reels, In-stream | Engagement, storytelling |
| Carousel (2–10 cards) | Feed | Product catalog, steps, features |
| Collection | Feed | Ecommerce product discovery |
| Stories/Reels | Stories, Reels | Full-screen, mobile-first |

## Funnel Structure
```
TOF (Top of Funnel) — Awareness
  Objective: Reach, Video Views, Brand Awareness
  Audience: Cold (Interest, Lookalike 3–10%)
  Creative: Educational, entertaining, no hard sell

MOF (Middle of Funnel) — Consideration
  Objective: Traffic, Engagement, Lead Generation
  Audience: Warm (video viewers, page engagers, website visitors 30–90d)
  Creative: Social proof, testimonials, benefits

BOF (Bottom of Funnel) — Conversion
  Objective: Conversions, Catalog Sales, Messages
  Audience: Hot (website visitors 7–30d, cart abandoners, customer list)
  Creative: Offer-driven, urgency, direct CTA
```

## Key Settings
```
Frequency cap: limit impressions per user per time window
  Recommended: <3 per week for cold audiences (ad fatigue above)

Attribution window:
  1-day click: conservative, best for impulse purchases
  7-day click: standard, captures longer consideration
  7-day click + 1-day view: includes view-through conversions
```

## A/B Testing Methodology
- Test one variable at a time (creative, audience, placement, offer)
- Statistical significance: ≥95% confidence before declaring winner
- Minimum runtime: 7 days (avoid weekend/weekday bias)
- Budget: enough for 50+ results per variant for reliable data
- Use Meta's built-in A/B test tool (splits traffic 50/50 at auction level)

## Performance Benchmarks
- CPM: $7–15 (varies by audience, industry, season)
- CTR (link): 0.5–2.0%
- ROAS ecommerce: 2–5× (varies by margin and funnel maturity)
