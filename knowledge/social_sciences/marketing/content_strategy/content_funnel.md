# Content Funnel Strategy

## TOFU — Top of Funnel (Awareness)
```
Goal: attract broad audience, build brand visibility
Audience: cold, problem-unaware or problem-aware
```
**Content types**: blog posts, educational videos, infographics, podcasts, social media posts, SEO-driven guides
**Metrics**: traffic, impressions, reach, new visitors, social shares
**CTA**: "Learn more", "Subscribe", "Follow"

Content criteria: answers "what" and "why" questions; no product mention.

## MOFU — Middle of Funnel (Consideration)
```
Goal: nurture interest, educate on solutions, build trust
Audience: warm, solution-aware, evaluating options
```
**Content types**: case studies, webinars, comparison guides, email sequences, whitepapers, templates, free tools
**Metrics**: email signups, webinar registrations, time on page, return visits, downloads
**CTA**: "Download the guide", "Join the webinar", "Get the template"

Content criteria: shows how your approach solves the problem; introduces brand differentiation.

## BOFU — Bottom of Funnel (Decision)
```
Goal: convert to purchase or qualified lead
Audience: hot, product-aware, ready to buy or close to it
```
**Content types**: free trials, demos, product comparisons, customer testimonials, ROI calculators, pricing pages, FAQ
**Metrics**: conversion rate, demo requests, trials started, SQLs, revenue
**CTA**: "Start free trial", "Book a demo", "Buy now"

Content criteria: removes objections, provides proof, reduces risk (money-back, free trial).

## Content Scoring Model
```
Content Score = Traffic weight × Engagement weight × Conversion weight

Example weights:
  Traffic:    0.3 (normalized monthly sessions)
  Engagement: 0.3 (avg time on page, scroll depth, comments)
  Conversion: 0.4 (leads or revenue attributed)
```
Rank all content by score quarterly → identify: update (high potential, low score), promote (high score, low traffic), consolidate (low score, thin content).

## Content Calendar Framework
```
80/20 rule: 80% educational/entertaining, 20% promotional
Pillar content: 1 major piece/month (guide, report, video series)
Supporting content: 4–8 pieces derived from each pillar
Distribution: publish → repurpose → promote → update
```

## SEO + Content Alignment
- TOFU: informational keywords (how, what, why)
- MOFU: commercial keywords (best, top, comparison, alternatives to)
- BOFU: transactional keywords (buy, pricing, free trial, demo)

## Content Decay
- Most blog posts lose 50–70% of traffic within 12–18 months without updates
- Refreshing top 20% of content by traffic = highest ROI content activity
- Update signals: new data, changed SERPs, outdated screenshots, broken links
