## Core Insight
We study the Hall viscosity of the quark gluon plasma (QGP) created in non-central heavy-ion collisions. In the presence of a strong magnetic field or vorticity, rotational symmetry is broken from O(3) to O(2), allowing for two independent Hall viscosities associated with shear deformations transverse and parallel to the symmetry-breaking direction.
