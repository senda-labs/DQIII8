# Natural Sciences

This centroid covers empirical study of the natural world through observation
and experimentation. It deals with physical, chemical, and biological phenomena.

## Agents
- Physics: mechanics, thermodynamics, electromagnetism, quantum mechanics, optics
- Chemistry: organic, inorganic, biochemistry, materials science, reactions
- Biology: cell biology, genetics, ecology, evolution, anatomy, physiology
  - Nutrition: diet planning, macronutrients, micronutrients, BMR, TDEE, meal plans
  - Genetics: DNA, gene therapy, CRISPR, heredity, genomics
- Earth Sciences: geology, meteorology, oceanography, climate science

## When this centroid activates
- User asks about diet, nutrition, meal plans, calories, protein, vitamins
- User asks about physics, chemistry, biology concepts
- User asks about health, body composition, supplements
- Keywords: diet, nutrition, calories, protein, BMR, physics, chemistry, biology, gene
