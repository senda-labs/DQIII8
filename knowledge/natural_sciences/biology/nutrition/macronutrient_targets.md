# Macronutrient Targets

## Caloric Density
```
Protein:     4 kcal/g
Carbohydrate: 4 kcal/g
Fat:          9 kcal/g
Alcohol:      7 kcal/g (no nutritional value)
```

## Protein Targets (g/kg body weight/day)
| Goal | Target | Source |
|------|--------|--------|
| RDA minimum | 0.8 g/kg | WHO/DRI |
| Maintenance | 1.0–1.2 g/kg | General health |
| Fat loss | 1.6–2.4 g/kg | Preserve LBM |
| Muscle gain | 1.6–2.2 g/kg | ISSN position stand |
| Endurance athletes | 1.4–1.7 g/kg | ISSN |
| Strength athletes | 1.6–2.0 g/kg | ISSN |
| Maximum useful | ~2.5 g/kg | Beyond = no added benefit |

## Carbohydrate Targets
```
Low carb:            <130 g/day or <26% of calories
Moderate (USDA):      45–55% of total calories
Endurance athletes:   6–10 g/kg/day
Strength athletes:    4–7 g/kg/day
Minimum brain needs:  130 g/day (glucose)
```

## Fat Targets
```
Minimum (hormones):   20% of calories
Recommended range:    25–35% of calories
```
- Essential fatty acids (omega-3, omega-6) cannot be synthesized
- Below 20% fat: testosterone and estrogen production impaired

## Caloric Adjustments by Goal
| Goal | Adjustment | Expected Rate |
|------|------------|---------------|
| Fat loss | –300 to –500 kcal/day | 0.3–0.5 kg/week |
| Muscle gain | +200 to +400 kcal/day | 0.2–0.4 kg/week |
| Recomposition | ±0 (maintenance) | Slow, requires high protein ≥2.0 g/kg |
| Aggressive cut | –500 to –750 kcal/day | 0.5–0.75 kg/week |

## Macro Priority Order (practical)
1. Set protein (goal-specific g/kg)
2. Set fat (minimum 20%, aim 25–30%)
3. Fill remaining calories with carbohydrates
