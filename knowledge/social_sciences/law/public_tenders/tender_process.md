# Tender Process — From Expediente to Contract Award

## Full Process Flow
```
1. Necesidad → justificación (art. 28 LCSP)
2. Expediente de contratación (pliego condiciones)
3. Supervisión / fiscalización (Intervención)
4. Aprobación del gasto
5. Anuncio de licitación (PLACSP + DOUE if SARA)
6. Presentación de ofertas (3 sobres / lotes)
7. Apertura de sobres (Mesa de Contratación)
8. Evaluación de criterios
9. Propuesta de adjudicación
10. Adjudicación provisional → 15 días alegaciones
11. Adjudicación definitiva (resolución motivada)
12. Formalización del contrato (not before 15-day standstill)
```

## The 3-Envelope System (Sobre/Plica)
| Envelope | Content | When opened |
|----------|---------|-------------|
| Sobre A — Administrativo | DEUC, legal capacity docs, solvency evidence, guarantees | First |
| Sobre B — Técnico (juicio de valor) | Technical proposal, methodology, team CVs, case studies | Second (before economic) |
| Sobre C — Económico (automáticos) | Price offer, delivery time, quantifiable criteria | Last (after scoring B) |

Strict separation prevents price from influencing technical evaluation.

## Procedures and Deadlines

### Procedimiento Abierto (Open Procedure)
```
Standard (SARA contracts):      minimum 35 days from publication to deadline
Simplified (VEC < SARA):        minimum 15 days
Supersimplificado (VEC ≤ 35,000€ services / ≤ 40,000€ works): no formal process
```

### Procedimiento Restringido (Restricted Procedure)
```
Phase 1 — Selection: minimum 30 days to submit candidacies
Phase 2 — Invitation: minimum 15 days to submit full offers
Used when: pre-qualification needed, limited number of invitees (min 5, max defined in pliego)
```

### Procedimiento Negociado (Negotiated Procedure)
- Without publicity: VEC below thresholds (works <1M€ if justified, services <100K€)
- With publicity: complex contracts where open procedure not adequate
- Minimum 3 candidates invited (with publication)

### Diálogo Competitivo (Competitive Dialogue)
- For highly complex contracts where authority cannot define solution
- Authority dialogues with shortlisted candidates before inviting full offers
- Common for: major infrastructure PPP, complex IT systems

### Asociación para la Innovación (Innovation Partnership)
- For R&D contracts where solution does not yet exist in market
- Combined development + supply phase

## Mesa de Contratación
Composition: president + secretary + at least 3 voting members (including legal and economic advisors)
Functions:
- Verify administrative envelope (qualification phase)
- Score technical envelopes (juicio de valor)
- Open and score economic envelopes
- Issue adjudication proposal (non-binding on contracting authority)

## Standstill Period (Período de Suspensión)
```
Minimum 15 calendar days between adjudication notification and contract formalization
```
Purpose: allows unsuccessful tenderers to challenge award before contract is signed.
Ignoring standstill = grounds for contract nullity (art. 39 LCSP).

## Key Deadlines Post-Award
- Formalización: within 15 days after standstill (if no challenge)
- Garantía definitiva: 5% of contract value (10% if temerarious bid accepted)
- Acta de comprobación de replanteo (works): within 30 days of formalization
