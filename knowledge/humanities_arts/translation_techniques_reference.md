# Translation Techniques Reference

## Vinay & Darbelnet's 7 Procedures

| # | Technique | Definition | EN → ES Example |
|---|-----------|-----------|-----------------|
| 1 | Borrowing (préstamo) | Use source word unchanged | software → software; marketing → marketing |
| 2 | Calque | Literal structure translation; new compound | skyscraper → rascacielos; football → fútbol americano |
| 3 | Literal translation | Word-for-word; grammatical structure preserved | The book is on the table → El libro está en la mesa |
| 4 | Transposition | Change grammatical category; meaning preserved | He swam across the river → Cruzó el río a nado (verb → adverb) |
| 5 | Modulation | Change point of view / perspective | It's not hard → Es fácil; The glass is half empty → el vaso está medio lleno |
| 6 | Equivalence | Culturally equivalent expression; non-literal | It's raining cats and dogs → Llueve a cántaros |
| 7 | Adaptation | Cultural substitution; target culture equivalent | Baseball analogy → fútbol analogy in generic sports context |

**Hierarchy of procedures:** Literal (when possible) → Transposition → Modulation → Equivalence → Adaptation (last resort, highest domestication)
