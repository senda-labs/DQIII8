# DQ Demo Prompts — Try These First

## 1. Business Plan (multi-domain: social + applied + humanities)
dq "Create a complete business plan for a specialty coffee shop in London
targeting remote workers, including financial projections and marketing strategy"

## 2. Portfolio Risk Analysis (social > finance)
dq "Analyze the risk profile of a portfolio with 60% S&P 500, 25% bonds,
10% gold, 5% crypto. Calculate expected VaR at 95% confidence"

## 3. Technical Architecture (applied > software)
dq "Design a microservices architecture for a food delivery app that handles
10,000 concurrent orders, with authentication, payment, and real-time tracking"

## 4. Creative Writing (humanities > literature)
dq "Write chapter 1 of a sci-fi novel where Earth discovers that gravity
is artificially maintained by an ancient alien infrastructure"

## 5. Research Analysis (natural + formal)
dq "Explain the latest approaches to protein folding prediction and how
AlphaFold changed the field, with key equations and metrics"
