# Solvency Requirements (Solvencia)

## Legal Basis
Arts. 74–91 LCSP. Contracting authorities must define solvency criteria in the pliego (PCAP).
Criteria must be: related to contract object, proportional to VEC, non-discriminatory.

## Economic and Financial Solvency (art. 87 LCSP)

### Turnover (Volumen de Negocio)
```
Maximum required: annual turnover ≤ 1.5× VEC
(or ≤ 1.5× annual VEC for multi-year contracts)
```
Evidenced by: audited accounts (cuentas anuales), tax declarations (modelo 200/303).

### Other Economic Solvency Criteria
- **Net equity / patrimonio neto**: positive balance sheet equity
- **Professional indemnity insurance (seguro de responsabilidad civil)**: coverage ≥ defined amount in pliego (typically 10–30% VEC)
- **Debt ratios**: authority may require specific leverage thresholds

## Technical and Professional Solvency

### Works Contracts (art. 88 LCSP)
```
Minimum: list of works completed in the last 5 years
At least 1 work of same nature and category ≥ 50–70% of VEC
(exact % set by contracting authority in pliego)
```
Evidence: certificates from contracting authorities (certificados de buena ejecución) stating:
- Object, amount, dates, location
- Whether works were carried out to satisfactory standard

### Services Contracts (art. 90 LCSP)
```
Minimum: list of principal services performed in last 3 years
(1 year for works-related services: last 5 years)
At least 1 service of same nature ≥ 50–70% of annual VEC
```
Evidence: certificates from clients (public or private); if private, declaration by company.

### Supplies Contracts (art. 89 LCSP)
- Deliveries in last 3 years (same type)
- Technical staff/facilities available
- Quality control systems (ISO 9001 or equivalent)

### Team and Resources
- CVs of key technical personnel (titulaciones, professional experience)
- Technical equipment and tools available for execution
- Quality management certifications (ISO 9001, ISO 14001)

## ROLECE — Registro Oficial de Licitadores y Empresas Clasificadas del Estado
- Centralised registry of companies accredited to tender for public contracts
- Valid registration replaces submission of solvency documents in every tender
- Updated: company must notify changes within 15 days
- Access: https://contrataciondelestado.es

### ROLECE vs. RELI
- ROLECE: national registry (AGE contracts)
- RELI (Registros de Licitadores de las CCAA): regional registries (CCAA contracts)
- Both recognised across all administrations

## Clasificación Empresarial (Grupos, Subgrupos, Categorías)
```
Mandatory for:
  Works contracts:    VEC ≥ 500,000 €
  Services contracts: VEC ≥ 500,000 € (only if classified group exists for that service type)
```
- Granted by: Junta Consultiva de Contratación Pública del Estado
- Structure: Group (letter A–U) → Subgroup (number) → Category (1–8 by contract value)
- Category amounts (works):
  | Category | Anualidad media |
  |----------|----------------|
  | 1 | ≤ 60,000 € |
  | 2 | ≤ 120,000 € |
  | 3 | ≤ 360,000 € |
  | 4 | ≤ 840,000 € |
  | 5 | ≤ 2,400,000 € |
  | 6 | > 2,400,000 € |

- Clasificación replaces solvency proof for covered works/services
- Valid for 3 years; must be renewed before expiry

## Provisional and Definitive Guarantees (Garantías)
```
Garantía provisional: 3% VEC (optional, increasingly rare)
Garantía definitiva:  5% adjudication price (excl. VAT)
                      10% if temerarious bid accepted
```
Form: cash deposit, bank guarantee (aval), insurance bond (seguro de caución).
