# Digital Marketing Fundamentals

## Definition
Digital marketing is the promotion of products, services, or brands through digital channels including search engines, social media, email, and websites. It enables precise targeting, real-time measurement, and rapid iteration compared to traditional marketing.

## Core Concepts

- **SEO (Search Engine Optimization):** Improving website visibility in organic search results. On-page SEO: keyword optimization, title tags, meta descriptions, content quality. Off-page SEO: backlinks, domain authority. Technical SEO: site speed, mobile-friendliness, structured data, crawlability.
- **SEM (Search Engine Marketing):** Paid advertising on search engines, primarily Google Ads and Bing Ads. Pay-per-click (PPC) model. Quality Score determines ad rank and CPC. Key metrics: impression share, CTR, conversion rate, ROAS.
- **Social Media Marketing:** Building brand presence and engagement on platforms — Instagram, LinkedIn, TikTok, YouTube, X. Organic (content, community) vs. paid (boosted posts, ads). Platform-specific content strategy.
- **Content Marketing:** Creating valuable, relevant content (blogs, videos, infographics, podcasts) to attract and retain a target audience. Builds authority and generates organic traffic. Content funnel: TOFU (awareness) → MOFU (consideration) → BOFU (decision).
- **Email Marketing:** Direct communication with subscribers. Highest ROI channel. Segmentation, personalization, and automation (welcome sequences, drip campaigns, cart abandonment). Key metrics: open rate, CTR, unsubscribe rate.
- **Analytics and Attribution:** GA4, Adobe Analytics track user behavior. Attribution models (first-touch, last-touch, multi-touch, data-driven) assign credit to channels. Funnel analysis, cohort analysis, LTV modeling.
- **Conversion Rate Optimization (CRO):** A/B testing, heatmaps, user session recordings to improve landing pages and funnels.

## Key Metrics
- CTR (Click-Through Rate): clicks / impressions
- Conversion Rate: conversions / visitors
- CAC (Customer Acquisition Cost): spend / new customers
- ROAS (Return on Ad Spend): revenue / ad spend
- LTV (Lifetime Value): avg revenue per customer over their lifetime
- NPS (Net Promoter Score): customer satisfaction and loyalty

## Practical Applications
- Launch campaigns: define audience → create content → choose channels → set budget → measure → optimize.
- A/B testing: test headline, CTA, image, or landing page variants.
- Funnel optimization: awareness → consideration → conversion → retention → advocacy.
- Influencer marketing: leverage creators for authentic reach.
